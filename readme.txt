Q)What is ChatBot?
A chatbot is an inteligent piece of software that is capable of communicating and performing actions similar to a human.Chatbot  are used a lot in customer interaction, marketing on social network sites and instantly messaging the client.
There are two basic types of Chatbot models based on how they are built:

1) Retrieval based model:
A retrieval-based chatbot uses predifined input patterns and responses.It then uses some type of heuristic apporach to select the appropriate response.It is widely used in the industry to make goal-oriented chatbots where we can customize the tone and flow of the chatbot to drive our customers with the best experience.

2) Genrative based model:
Generative models are not based on some predifined responses.
